# Family Features Guide

The **Family** tab is the social hub of the HealthTracker app. It allows you to create groups, compete with friends and family, and track each other's progress.

## 1. Getting Started
When you first open the Family tab, you have two options:
*   **Create New Family**: Enter a name (e.g., "The Glass Family") to start a new group. You will become the first member.
*   **Join a Family**: Enter the **6-digit Invite Code** shared by a family member to join an existing group.

## 3. How Data is Shared
*   **Privacy First**: The app only shares your *daily totals* (Steps, Calories, etc.). It does **not** share your location, heart rate, or minute-by-minute activity.
*   **Syncing**: Data is synced automatically when you open the app. If the leaderboard looks out of date, pull down to refresh!
